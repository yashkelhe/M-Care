About us



M-Care Sustainability is a forward-thinking initiative dedicated to revolutionizing menstrual waste management with a strong commitment to environmental sustainability. Our mission is to create a positive impact on the planet by addressing a crucial but often overlooked issue: the ecological footprint of menstruation.


At M-Care Sustainability, we believe that the menstrual waste generated by disposable pads and tampons can be transformed into valuable resources. Eco Flow invites you to join us in this menstrual waste reuse revolution. Together, we can transform the way society views and manages menstrual waste, paving the way for a more sustainable and environmentally friendly future. Make a difference with M-Care today.
